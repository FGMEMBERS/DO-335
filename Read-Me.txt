A little history
================
In 1944 the record of the official world speed for a piston apparatus was held by Heinkel He-110 with 746 km/h.
The same year Dornier Do 335 was capable of a maximum speed of 765 km/h to 6500 meters.
It could go up to 8000 meters in 14,5 seconds. Thus it outclassed all the allied hunters which were contemporary for him.
